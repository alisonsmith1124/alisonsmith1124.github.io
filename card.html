Seahorse

Seahorses are famous for their monogamous relationships and male pregnancies.

  • Scientific Name: Hippocampus
  • Average Size: 2-4 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 1-4 years
  • Habitat: Ocean

A female seahorse lays dozens, sometimes hundreds, of eggs in a pouch on the male seahorse’s abdomen. Called a ‘brood pouch’, it’s a bit like the pouch of a kangaroo, used for carrying young.
